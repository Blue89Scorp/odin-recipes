Jalapeno Salsa

This is a fresh homemade salsa, with a kick!!

This salsa has a nice heat from Jalapenos that's sure to be a hit at the party!!

Ingredients

  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup finely diced onion
  • 5 Jalapeno peppers, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons lime juice

Direstions

  1. In a medium bowl, stir together tomatoes, onion, Jalapeno peppers, cilantro, salt, and lime juice.
  2. Chill for one hour in Refrigerator before serving.
  3. Serve in dipping bowl or as a topping on your favorite dish and enjoy!!
